My Projects

Final Essay

In Writing and Critical Thinking, I wrote a final essay evaluating two candidates for the Ontario Tech University Board of Governors, Jordan Jacobs (AI and technology) and Ahmad Ghahreman (sustainability and recycling). I based my evalutaions on our universities specific priorities like student success, innovation, industry partnerships, or Co-op, etc. Based on this, Jordan Jacobs is the better fit due to his expertise in AI innovation, and his goals align with our university's priorities.

Grade Book project

In Object Oriented Programming, I created python code that would emulate a teachers gradebook for a student. In this code, you could create a student by making their student id and linking it with their name, then you can enroll them into a class and enter a grade for them. My code also wouldnt let you enroll a student for a class that they were already in, as well as it won't let you enter a grade for a student that wasn't in a certian class, or didn't exist.

AI Chatbot Report Draft

I wrote a report draft as if I were working for Meta (aka Facebook and Instagram), proposing the idea of using AI chatbots to more efficiently deal with customer service and fix long wait times. My final report evaluates Rule-Based AI, Machine Learning AI, and Hybrid AI-Human chatbot models. My recommendation was to use Hybrid AI-Human chatbot models due to their low operational costs and due to Hybrid models being the most efficient in responding to customers.
